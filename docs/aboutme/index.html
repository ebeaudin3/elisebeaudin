About Me



Bonjour/Hi!

My name is Élise Beaudin, I am a physical oceanographer, and a storyteller. I have a lot of sea-going experience, as well as data analysis skills of anything ocean and climate data related.

Throughout my life, I have lived and worked in several countries: Canada, Sweden, Germany, Italy, and the USA. I love programming, playing and composing music, filmmaking, and of course the ocean!

Feel free to connect with me via Linkedin, or contact me via email.
